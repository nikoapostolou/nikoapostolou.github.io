Marketing Mix Modeling

Media Mix Model with Diminishing Returns & Adstock

Built a marketing mix model in Python to quantify the impact of paid search, paid social, display, and email on conversions. Included channel-level saturation curves and adstock to capture carryover effects.

  • Estimated channel ROI and marginal ROI for budget reallocation.
  • Simulated “what-if” spend scenarios for future planning.
  • Stack: Python, pandas, statsmodels, Matplotlib.

Attribution · NLP

URL Similarity Model for Broken Ad Destinations

Developed an NLP-based similarity model to automatically suggest replacement URLs when ad landing pages returned “404 – Page Not Found”, protecting paid campaigns from wasted spend.

  • Used embeddings + cosine similarity to match to the closest live page.
  • Deployed as a pipeline to scan URLs and flag risky destinations.
  • Reduced potential lost spend by dynamically routing traffic.
